DEPARTED

A data visualization of leading causes of death in the 20th century.


The visualization clearly indicates non communicable and infectious diseases to have claimed more lives in the 20th century, than any another.

- Hover over each circle for more detail.
- Circle size/radius indicates the number of deaths in comparison to others. Therefore bigger circles are large death counts and smaller circles are lower death counts.


This visualization was compiled in response to Condé Nast's data visualization challenge.
Data Source: Condé Nast
